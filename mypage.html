The Easiest Way to Make a 1-2-3-4 Cake

Inside every human being there is a little chef who longs to cook something, but sometimes the talent for cooking is missing. This recipe does not require talent, but rather a passion to sweets. It is essential to present recipes that is engaging, fast, and easy to make. This ensures that the little chef inside us remain interested in the food. 1-2-3-4 Cake is one of the basic cake recipes. It does not require much of time to make, few ingredients, and easy steps. As you can see, its name is 1-2-3-4 Cake , and that come from the amounts of the four main ingredients butter, sugar, flour, and eggs. It can be prepared with different flavors and shapes, but today I will show you the original flavor, which is 1-2-3-4 Vanilla Cake. It is an old-fashioned yellow cake you will make it again and again.

Below is the ingredients that you will need them to make 1-2-3-4 Cake:

  • 1 cup of cooking oil or unsalted butter
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 3 cups of flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • water as needed/milk as needed

The steps that you need to follow:

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°. Grease and flour the cake pan you will use.
  2. In a large bowl, beat oil/butter and sugar with a mixer at medium speed until fluffy, 3 to 4 minutes.
  3. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Add the vanilla to the mixture.
  5. In a medium bowl, stir together dry ingredients(the flower and baking powder).
  6. Gradually add 1/2 the flower mixture to liquid mixture, and once it disappears add the other half.
  7. Add a small amount of water/milk as needed.
  8. Pour the mixture into prepared pan.
  9. Bake until a wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean, 28 to 30 minutes.
  10. Let it cool in pans for 10 minutes. Remove from pans, and let cool completely on wire racks.
